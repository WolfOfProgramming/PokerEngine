About Project

This project was designed to design a universal Poker engine that simulates the simplified behavior of a real player. There are only 3 options in this version of Poker, you can raise, fold, or call. The person with the rarer set of cards wins. Layouts as well as individual card values are checked. I hope you will have a good time.

Game Rules

The rules of the game are simple. In each game, the player receives 5 cards that he can exchange. The computer also receives 5 cards from the same pool. After exchanging a player's cards, he has the option of folding, raising the stake, or checking the computer. After the game, the player has the option of returning to the home page or playing again.
